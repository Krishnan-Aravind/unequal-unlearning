Not All Data Are Unlearned Equally

Aravind Krishnan♣, Siva Reddy♦, Marius Mosbach♦

♣ Saarland University  |  ♦ Mila – Quebec AI Institute, McGill University

Overview

We investigate how the strength of a model’s beliefs affects its ability to unlearn data, showing that data that is more frequent in the pretraining corpus is not forgotten easily.
